Famous Places to visit in India

Tamil Nadu

Tamil Nadu is known for its rich culture and traditions; ancient temples; delectable food; stunning beaches; opulent art and craft; beautiful hill stations. It is one of the top places to visit in the world.

The Taj Mahal

Taj Mahal is one of the Seven Wonders of the World. Taj is a symbol of the great Mughal heritage of India. Experiencing Taj is like a journey back to the magnificent Mughal Empire. Taj Mahal is a monument of love.

Kerala

Popularly known as "God's Own Country". It has rich cultural heritage, tropical climate, palm fringed beaches, misty hill stations, enchanting art forms which promise an unforgettable experience to travellers.

Goa

Goa attracts many international tourists. Goa has one of the best nightlife in India with trendy bars, beach shacks,elegant cafes,clubs and discotheques. Goa is also great for younger tourists with tighter pockets

Karnataka

Karnataka, the third most popular state in the country for tourism. Tourism centres on the ancient sculptured temples,the palaces, modern cities, the hill ranges, forests and beaches.

Hyderabad

Hyderabad is the capital of the South Indian state of Telangana. The city is noted for its monuments which includes the masterpiece of Charminar , the fort of Golconda and the museums.
